Before sending your email, please check that your question has not been answered below. If your question has not been answered, please click here to find contact details for the relevant department.


1) What is the Official Membership scheme?

The Official Manchester United Membership Scheme is a seasonal membership which allows you to get closer to the world's greatest team. Disabled Supporters should visit the Manchester United Disabled Supporters' Association website as there is a host of information available here including a specific FAQ section.

2) Who owns the Club?

Manchester United plc., a company incorporated in the Cayman Islands, is the ultimate parent company of Manchester United Football Club. Manchester United plc is listed on the New York Stock Exchange. The only person holding 10% or more of any class of shares in Manchester United plc is the Glazer family.

4) Can I have a signed item to auction for charity?

Applications are being accepted from 1st August 2014 for the 2014-2015 season. All requests of this nature are required to be submitted via our website www.mufoundation.org/charityrequests. Due to the volume of requests we receive, we require at least 6 weeks notice prior to your event. If your event does not fall within this timescale unfortunately, we will not be able to help.

7) Can my child attend a match on their own?

A child must be 12 years old or over to be able to attend a match unaccompanied. An under 12 child cannot attend on their own. They must have a responsible person aged 16 or over with them.

8) How do I advertise at Manchester United?

It is the club's policy that the right to advertise on ManUtd.com, in Manchester United media and on pitchside perimeter boards at Old Trafford is only afforded to the club's official partners as part of a long-term and broader partnership. It is not possible to purchase this advertising space. Furthermore, as you would appreciate, as one of the world's leading football clubs a significant investment is required to become an official partner of the club. If you feel your organisation is in a position to consider a broad and long-term partnership, please do not hesitate to get in touch with the club via sponsorship@manutd.co.uk.
